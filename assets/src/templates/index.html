Transforming Access To Pharmacy Benefits

Medications play a vital role in people's lives. When taken as prescribed, they can be lifesaving and life changing. However, many Americans struggle with the cost of prescription drugs. At Navitus, this is personal to us.

We exist to make medications more affordable for the people who need them.

Our pass-through model ensures that plan sponsors and health plans are billed only for the net cost of medications and that they get 100% of all discounts and rebates we receive. We take pride in providing our clients with full visibility into their pharmacy-benefit costs, value-added savings opportunities and enabling them to offer additional benefits to members when they save. Most importantly, we do what we do for the right reasons: to help members afford the medicine they need so that they can live their lives more fully.

As we look toward the future, we remain dedicated to this mission and I invite you to explore the results that Navitus delivered in 2022.

Brent Eberle

Chief Pharmacy Officer, Navitus Health Solutions
